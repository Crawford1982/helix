About This Guide

Why this site exists, how it is written, and what kind of help it is meant to give visitors planning a trip to the Falkirk Helix and The Kelpies.

Home › About the guide
Independent site

FalkirkHelix.co.uk is an independent visitor guide. It is not the official website for the Helix, The Kelpies, Falkirk Council, or any event organiser.

This site is built for people who want practical answers quickly. Most visitors do not need marketing copy. They want honest help deciding where to park, how long to allow, whether the tour is worth paying for, and how to fit the Helix into a better day out.

What the Site Tries to Do Well

Prioritise usefulness

Pages are written to answer the practical questions people actually ask before visiting, not just to repeat the broad background story.

Be clear about uncertainty

When a detail is likely to change, such as booking rules, events, or seasonal operations, the site treats that as planning guidance rather than pretending it is a live operational source.

Help visitors make better choices

The best local guides do more than describe a place. They help people decide what is worth doing and how to have a better visit.

How to Use the Information Here

Use this guide for orientation, planning, and common-sense trip building. If a detail affects money, access, timing, or travel decisions on a particular day, treat the page as a starting point and verify any time-sensitive information with the relevant official source before you travel.

Editorial Approach

The copy on this site is intentionally direct. That is on purpose. Visitors often want practical confidence more than promotional language. The goal is to be clear, useful, and realistic about who The Kelpies are best for, what makes the Helix a stronger visit, and when expectations should be adjusted.
