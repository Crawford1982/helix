Kelpies Live 2026

Kelpies Live returns to the Great Lawn at Helix Park in June 2026, bringing major international acts to the heart of Central Scotland. The concert series has become one of the biggest outdoor music events in the Falkirk area.

Event Times

Gates typically open at 5pm on Fridays and 2pm on Saturdays. Music is scheduled to end between 10pm and 10:30pm. Check your specific event ticket for exact timings.

Tickets

Tickets are available through the official Kelpies Live website. Options include general admission, VIP (with access to a dedicated area near the front, exclusive bar and food vendor), and grandstand seated tickets.

Some dates have sold out — check availability early if you want to go.

Accommodation Tip

Hotels in Falkirk fill up quickly during Kelpies Live week. If you're coming from further afield, book accommodation well in advance. See where to stay →

Event Day Information

Getting There

Helix Park is accessible by car, train, and bus. During Kelpies Live events, traffic and parking arrangements may differ from normal. Event-specific parking and transport details are usually published closer to the date. General directions to the Helix →

What to Bring

  • Your printed or digital ticket
  • Layers — Scottish evenings can turn cool quickly
  • Waterproof jacket (it's Scotland, be prepared)
  • Comfortable shoes — you'll be on grass
  • Cash and cards — food and drink vendors are on site

Food & Drink

Food vendors and bars are available inside the event grounds. VIP ticket holders get access to an exclusive bar and food vendor. Outside food and drink policies vary by event — check the specific event terms.

Other Events at the Helix

Beyond Kelpies Live, the Helix hosts various events throughout the year:

  • Parkrun: A free weekly 5km run through the park (Saturdays, 9:30am)
  • Seasonal events: Halloween, Christmas, and other themed events for families
  • Light Up Our Landmarks: Scottish Canals occasionally lights The Kelpies in special colours for charity campaigns and national events
  • Community events: Local charity runs, cycling events, and community gatherings

Visiting During Events

During major events like Kelpies Live, normal access to The Kelpies and the surrounding area may be restricted. Car parks may be closed to general visitors. If you're planning a regular visit, check what's on before you come — especially during June.
