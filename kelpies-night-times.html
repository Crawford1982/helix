Kelpies at Night Opening Times

What actually stays open after dark, and what to check before you go.

Home › Kelpies Night Opening Times

Quick answer

The sculptures and outdoor area are generally accessible after dark, with illuminations switching on around dusk. What changes is parking-gate and facility times, so always check those separately before an evening trip.

What "Open at Night" Really Means

Visitors often ask if The Kelpies are open at night. In practice, the outdoor experience is available, but indoor services and specific parking options may close earlier than expected.

Best Arrival Window

  • Arrive 6090 minutes before sunset.
  • Explore in daylight first, then stay for illumination.
  • This timing gives better photos and reduces rush/parking pressure.

Night Visit Checklist

  • Check current parking gate times.
  • Dress for wind and lower evening temperatures.
  • If travelling with children, plan food and facilities before full dark.
